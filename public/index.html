Why wood, why by hand

Three things mass production can't fake.

Acrylic and laser-cut MDF dominate this category because they're fast and cheap. We chose the slower material on purpose — here's what that buys you.

MATERIAL

Solid wood, not veneer

Sheesham and walnut sourced from local timber yards — pieces that age and deepen in color over years, the way real wood does, instead of yellowing plastic.

TECHNIQUE

Hand-finished edges

Every panel is sanded and oil-finished by hand after cutting. No sharp laser edges, no machine sheen — just a warm, tactile surface that photographs and feels premium.

PROCESS

Made to order, not stocked

We don't warehouse hundreds of identical pieces. Each order is cut for you, which means custom sizing, wording, and finish are the default, not an upcharge.

From timber to your wall

How a piece gets made.

STEP 1

You choose or customize

Pick a piece from the collection, or send us a verse, name, or size for something fully custom.

STEP 2

We cut and carve

Solid timber is cut to size, then hand- and machine-carved depending on the design's depth and detail.

STEP 3

Sanded and oil-finished

Every edge is hand-sanded, then treated with a natural oil finish that deepens the wood's grain.

STEP 4

Packed and shipped

Wrapped in protective padding and boxed for safe delivery anywhere in Pakistan, with care instructions.
